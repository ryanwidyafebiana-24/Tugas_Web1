The movie Robin Hood tells the story of a nobleman named Robin of Loxley, who returns from the Crusades to find his country oppressed by the corrupt Sheriff of Nottingham. He joins forces with a Moorish warrior named Little John to form a band of outlaws and fight against the Sheriff's tyranny. The movie features a reimagining of the classic legend with action-packed sequences and modern elements.
