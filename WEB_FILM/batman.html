"The Batman" (2022) is a neo-noir superhero film focusing on Batman's early years as a crimefighter in Gotham City. The plot centers around Batman investigating a series of murders committed by the Riddler, a serial killer targeting Gotham's elite and corrupt officials. As Batman delves deeper, he uncovers a web of corruption within the city, including his own family's involvement.
