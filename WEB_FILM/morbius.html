SUPERHERO


"Morbius" is a 2022 American superhero film based on the Marvel Comics character Morbius, the Living Vampire. It's part of Sony's Spider-Man Universe. The movie stars Jared Leto as Dr. Michael Morbius, a scientist who tries to cure himself of a rare blood disease by using experimental vampire bat DNA. The procedure gives him vampire-like abilities, but also a thirst for blood and a struggle with his own darkness.
