"Lightyear" is a 2022 American computer-animated science fiction action-adventure film produced by Pixar and distributed by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures. It's a spin-off of the "Toy Story" franchise, serving as an origin story for the character Buzz Lightyear, the astronaut who inspired the toy. The film follows Buzz, voiced by Chris Evans, as he attempts to return home after being marooned on a hostile planet with his commander and crew.
