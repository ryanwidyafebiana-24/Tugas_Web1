SUPERHERO


Spider-Man movies typically revolve around Peter Parker, a teenager who gains spider-like abilities after being bitten by a radioactive spider. He then uses these powers to fight crime as the superhero Spider-Man. The movies often explore themes of responsibility, loss, and the consequences of using great power. Many Spider-Man stories, especially in the MCU, feature Peter struggling to balance his normal life with his superhero duties, and often facing off against supervillains like the Green Goblin, Doctor Octopus, or Mysterio.
